About

Melita Ivković is a performing artist and educator with a career spanning three decades and over 500 performances across 32 countries.

Her recent significant collaboration has been the Croatian Guitar Duo with Edin Karamazov. In 2011, she co-founded Dina e Mel, an innovative ethnomusicological project reviving the forgotten songs of the Zadar Arbanasi. Their 2020 album Bërbili (Nightingale) was selected for the MOST – Balkan Music Export project in 2022. She is also a long-time member of the Zagreb Guitar Quartet.

Beyond performing, Melita has shaped the musical landscape through teaching, managing international festivals, and directing the Zadar Concert Office for 6 years. She currently teaches at the Elly Bašić Music School in Zagreb.
